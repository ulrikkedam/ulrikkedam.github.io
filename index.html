Smart Home Assistant Study

Short interaction study on how a smart-home assistant moves from asking to suggesting to acting.

Instructions

Please imagine that you are using a smart-home assistant in your home.

The assistant learns from repeated choices. Sometimes it asks. Sometimes it suggests. Sometimes it acts on its own.

Your task is simply to choose the response that feels most natural to you in each situation.
